ABOUT

Hello! I'm Tzu-Ling Tseng (曾子玲), a Product Designer with expertise in user research, prototyping, and visual design.

I excel in analysing user journeys and leveraging research data to identify and solve root problems, resulting in improved product user experiences and a 30% increase in overall activation.

Utilising a keen eye for branding and website design, I have successfully rebranded company websites, enhancing the design, usability, and overall user experience, resulting in a 10% increase in website traffic within just 4 months, and improved customer loyalty. Furthermore, my skills in collaborating with cross-functional teams have allowed me to streamline product development processes by 25%, resulting in more efficient and effective project timelines.

Additionally, with a growth mindset and commitment to continuous learning, I stay ahead of the curve with the latest design trends and technologies to deliver cutting-edge solutions for my clients or organisations.

I’m also passionate about film photography. I find inspiration in the unique colours and textures of black and white films, creating a sense of peace and calm in my photos.

Film photography is not just a hobby but a true passion that fuels my creativity and inspires my design work, always seeking new ways to incorporate unique aesthetics and storytelling into my projects. I'm excited to continue integrating my love for film photography into my designs, creating visually stunning and emotionally resonant user experiences.
